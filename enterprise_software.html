Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

(CRM) Customer Relationship Management is technology used for controlling company’s relationships and keeping in contact with new and existing customers. It is used to keep customers happy so that the business can grow. It is also used to help with customer services, marketing and sales industries. CRM helps the employer to focus on the company by keeping in contact with customers and suppliers. CRM will help you to find new customers and convening them to give you the business by supporting them and offering them additional services.

Supply Chain Management (S C M)

Is making sure that a business has just enough items at any one time. This is to minimise the amount of waste been produced and it also saves the company spend a lot of money at one time. It also helps to finish products in a timely manner to stop any delays, which helps to keep the customer happy. You can also track your delivery to see where your delivery is at any one time.
